Nevada-tan

The site nevada.natural20.net was an archive of Nevada-tan fanart and other images (including some photographs). It also had text summaries of the incident, along with the author's opinions on it and its news coverage. The domain is, as of 11 March 2015, suspended due to ICANN's policy change; I don't have the text, but I did save all 145 images. Download the whole set here, or view the gallery below.
