    >>21088
    i didn't render it, but made the mesh.
    i made a box to the height i wanted the supports, cut out the middle parts (using connect with edges set to 2), then made the X shape - and some triangular shapes to hide the seam where the X shapes met the longer poles, then i just duplicated them all the way up the length. and adjusted the longer poles afterwards.

    then it was a case of attaching everything and just duplicating and rotating the one piece to make all the others.

    it is rather messy and not something i would use for a game model (i would use the same technique but be less messy with the mesh).
